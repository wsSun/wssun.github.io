I am a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Yang Liu.

I received my Ph.D. from Nanjing University (NJU) in 2023, and was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhenyu Chen and Prof. Bin Luo. I also very luckily collaborated closely with Prof. Xiangyu Zhang (at Purdue University) and Assistant Prof. Guanhong Tao (at The University of Utah) during my Ph.D. journey.

My research interests mainly focus on Trustworthy Intelligent Software Engineering and AI Security.


Recent Services: ASE 2025, ICSE 2025 (Artifact Evaluation Track), ICLR 2025, FORGE 2025, QRS 2025, Guest Editor for Special Issue of Electronics: "Security and Privacy in Emerging Edge AI Systems and Applications" , Guest Editor for Special Issue of Electronics: "Large Language Models for Software Engineering and Software Applications" , Looking forward to your high-quality submissions!
